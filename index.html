Welcome to Novacom Digitronics Pvt Ltd

Novacom is a leader in the Indian Convergence Industry since 1997, specialising in providing all Broadband Products /Solutions. Novacom deals in world class analog and digital products used in Single and Two way transmissions on Cable, Satellite and Terrestrial Broadcast and Reception media. Apart from that, Novacom also deals with Luminaries like LED BULBS, TUBES and etc. Novacom Group is based in the city of Hyderabad, Andhra Pradesh, India the current beehive of the Convergence buzz and the most happening of cities in India and is logistically ideally, located to serve customers from all round our country and also the SAARC region.
